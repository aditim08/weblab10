Lord Krishna: The Eternal Melody

Lord Krishna, the embodiment of divine love and wisdom, enchants all beings with his flute, guiding souls toward peace and devotion.

“वासुदेवसुतं देवं कंसचाणूरमर्दनम् ।
देवकीपरमानन्दं कृष्णं वन्दे जगद्गुरुम् ॥”
Meaning: I bow to Lord Krishna — the son of Vasudeva, destroyer of Kansa and Chanura, supreme joy of Mother Devaki, and the eternal teacher of the universe.
